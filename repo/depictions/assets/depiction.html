Compatible with iOS 7, 8 and 9

Modify your lockscreen with Forton. A free lockscreen manipulation tweak. Now updated to support iphone 5s and above devices.

Forton currently offers options to:

1. Hide Emergency Button.
2. Hide Enter Passcode / Touch ID text.
3. Hide Cancel button on passcode screen.
4. Change Slide to Unlock text.
5. Remove lockscreen blur.
6. Remove lockscreen tint.
7. Allow or Disallow landscape lockscreen.
8. Speed up lockscreen animations.
9. Remove passcode attempts limit.
10. Remove lockscreen bounce.
11. Change Lockscreen Font Size.
12. Hide lockscreen slider.
13. Hide lockscreen date and time.
14. Change colour of lockscreen time.
15. Bold lockscreen time.

Configure options from Settings.
If you enable time in statusbar on lockscreen it is recommended that you disable the NC grabber for best results.
